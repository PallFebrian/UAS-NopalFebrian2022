Remap

Actually Remap the ECU Motor is a step taken to reset the ECU settings or Reflash ECU / ECU tuning which aims to calibrate the ECU in order to make the engine performance on the motor increase again.

Bore Up

Know What is Bore Up on a Motorcycle. Bore up is a modification made to increase the diameter of the default piston from the manufacturer. Later the piston will have a larger size. For example, a two-wheeled vehicle that has a piston with a diameter of 51 mm.

Porting

Porting polish itself is a method of smoothing the entry and exit of fuel to the engine. Simply put, porting polish can be said to modify or smooth the inlet hole to accelerate the rate of fuel and air into the combustion chamber.
